My Movie List

This small website is my way of organizing the movies I keep coming back to. Some of them are comfort films, some are the kind I recommend to friends, and a few are simply here because they left a strong impression on me after the credits rolled.

I wanted the pages to feel simple and easy to browse, so each section focuses on one thing: favorites, genres, and the order I would personally suggest if someone asked me where to start.

What this site shows

  • Links that connect one page to another
  • Unordered lists for grouped movie picks
  • Ordered lists for a watch sequence
  • A clear multi-page layout for GitHub Pages

Why I picked this topic

Movies are easy to organize in different ways. I can group them by personal favorites, sort them by genre, or arrange them in a viewing order depending on mood. That made this topic a good fit for links and lists.
