Green Bones

Green Bones is a Filipino drama film that stood out to me because it feels heavy, reflective, and character-driven. It is not the kind of movie that depends only on action or shock value. The story has a more serious tone, so it feels like something you pay attention to from start to finish.

Main actors:

  • Dennis Trillo
  • Ruru Madrid
  • Michael de Mesa
  • Alessandra de Rossi

Why I included it:

  1. It has a strong dramatic tone.
  2. The cast is solid.
  3. It feels like a movie you can discuss after watching.
