My top picks

  • Interstellar - I like how emotional it feels even with all the science-heavy scenes.
  • Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse - It is creative, energetic, and visually unforgettable.
  • Parasite - The story keeps shifting in a way that makes it hard to forget.
  • La La Land - I enjoy the music, the color, and the bittersweet ending.
  • The Dark Knight - It is one of those movies that still feels intense no matter how many times I watch it.
